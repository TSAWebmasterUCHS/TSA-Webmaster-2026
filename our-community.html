Our Purpose

The Union County Community Resource Hub exists to connect residents with important local services, events, and programs. Our goal is to make information accessible to families, newcomers, and everyone who lives in the community.

The People Behind It

Our team includes local volunteers, healthcare providers, educators, and community leaders who want to make Union County a stronger, healthier place to live.

Community Highlights

Some important resources in our community include:

  • Union General Hospital – medical care and health programs
  • Union County Public Library – educational programs and events
  • Union County Community Center – family activities and support services
